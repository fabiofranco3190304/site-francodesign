Rebranding ESJS - Mafra
A dynamic and modern new brand identity that reflects the young and creative environment of ESJS Mafra.

The relationship with the Palace of Mafra is something that is very rooted in this school, which is the main element of the current logo. This proposal intends to present a more modern and dynamic alternative to this representation.

This logo creates a bridge with the previous identity and with the village of Mafra.
